Erhan's Guide to the Galaxy

I am a researcher at Janelia Research Campus of Howard Hughes Medical Institute, where I am working on automated neuron reconstruction from large scale images as part of MOUSELIGHT. See RESEARCH page for what I am/was interested in.

I was a project lead at GE Global Research and adjunct professor at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute before joining to JRC.

I got my double B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Physics from METU in 2005, masters in ECE from Koc University in 2007 and PhD in ECE from Northeastern University in 2011.
